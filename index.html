Tech Interview Readiness Foundation

Interviews made easy

How can we improve our interview skills?

TIRF is a club at ASU that prepares students for technical interviews by teaching the data structures and algorithms that appear most frequently in interview questions.

Our members have received offers from tech companies like Flatiron Health, Epic Systems, Google, Bloomberg, and Facebook.

Who can join?

TIRF is open to students of all skill levels, whether you have never done a technical interview before, or are a seasoned expert.

Meetings are designed to benefit everyone, regardless of your skill level.
